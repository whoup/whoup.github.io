    Who Up App

    When you install the Who Up App you will be asked to provide us with your email, handle and a password (passwords cannot be seen by us whatsoever).

    User handles will only be seen by other users, your email address can only be seen by yourself.

    Online Status and Seen Status

    Other users will see your connection status (whether you are online or not – and if not, when was the last time you were connected to the service, i.e. you were up) as well as whether you saw messages they sent to you.

No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting.
